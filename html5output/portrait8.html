TRAVEL The ease of the Phoenix travel experience begins at the Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport, one of the most accessible and affordable major airports in the United States.  Sky Harbor does all the little things to keep travelers happy and comfortable.  Need Wi-Fi?  It's free in every terminal.  Hungry?  Dining options include some of the best local restaurants in the city. TRAVEL INFO The Phoenix Convention Center is a short 6.7 mile drive from the Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport (PHX). Transportation options include twelve airport car rental agencies, the METRO light rail ($2 each way), taxis ($15-$20 one way), and the SuperShuttle.
